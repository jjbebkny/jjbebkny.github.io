Art Thou Beautiful

by Jariah

I sit in an empty field with my mind in God’s heavenly hands and my heart free with the doves  Both separate from my flesh. Safe. Guarded.

I reach down and clutch the rich soil that is a storyteller to my soul. I have taken the curved branches of willow trees that used to weep for the limbs they had to carry and I have prayed for them

I have prayed for the field that I now sit on with memories soaked in blood buried 6 feet under  Like a potter I have shaped and transformed my pain with my pencil and she has done it with her paint brush and him with his hands as he plays the harp like David immersed in God’s goodness

My curves are art. The words that dance from my heart are art. The magic that my melanin radiates is art. The frequencies that I wine on are art. 

They run.  Not because they are afraid of the dark but because they are afraid of all this light.

Art thou beautiful?
