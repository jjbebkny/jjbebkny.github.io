EXPERIENCE
I WAS SENT
AWAY INTO
THIS NEW
LAND
My mind like the sea
filled with worry and anxiety
But God made it known that
He is with me.
So I began to walk and saw
that honestly it's not so bad.
But again when my thoughts
shouted louder than my prayers
I slipped and sank into my "bag"
LORD SAVE ME
I called out, beginning to drown in uncertainty, isolation and fear...
Why did I Doubt?
Worry...?
become Anxious...?
Could I not recall Psalm 4
after many nights of praying
and memorizing?
I came to KNOW my God once again,
and BELIEVE, so that now
my prayers are LOUD and POWERFUL,
so my thoughts are UNABLE to overcome.
