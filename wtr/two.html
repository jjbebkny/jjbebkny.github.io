I TRAVELED
TO A
NEW PLACE
There I was
thirsting, anticipating, waiting
to be poured into, to be filled
So I searched for something
to quench my thirst
Looking almost everywhere else
except the source
Now there are 3 things I am aware of:
MY STRUGGLES
I struggle with Vulnerability.
Being open about my faith,
feelings, my own self…
MY DESIRES
I desire to glorify God in all things.
Thus all things I do: design, ministry,
daily interactions, would all be platforms
for worship
MY ACTIONS
Therefore, have to, I must,
(and I can’t stress this enough)
read and reflect on his word
day and night…
Whatever it is that I do
That I desire
That I struggle with…
If the focus is not God
it would all be for nothing
I PRAY FOR A RENEWAL OF MY HEART
SO THAT I MAY FULLY EXPERIENCE
THE POURING IN
